About Us



Our History

ULS (Universal Logistics Services) was established in 2005, with a setup of an Office at Dehradun (Uttarakhand).
ULS became one of the largest Logistics Service provider for the Telecom/D2H & Electronics Industry in Uttarakhand.
Today, We are pleased to introduce ourselves as one of the leading and fast growing Cargo, Logistics and Warehousing Company in Uttrakhand and West UP.
We have our own strong network for operations spread throughout The Uttrakhand and West UP topology.


Our Infra

In our pursuit for excellence and our endeavor to provide better and economical services to our esteemed clients we are backed by:

  • 40+ Self Offices
  • Over 350 Destinations (WEST UP)
  • OVER 300 Destinations (UTTARAKHAND)
  • Over 400 delivery persons
We have a strong own network for Cargo, Logistics, Warehousing and 3PL services in Uttrakhand and West UP.
The group turnover for last FY has recorded over 10 crores.



Our Success

We have redefined logistics especially in on time delivery over the past '15 years' and continuously striving towards innovations.
Our 100% own roll and dedicated manpower backed by innovative concepts has proved very cost effective to our clients.
With a fascinating clientele, Universal enjoys substantial market leadership especially in ELECTRONIC CONSUMER DURABLES MARKET.

As we have already entered the 21st century, we may not be able to afford listening to words like :
Sorry, Misrouting, Confusion, Late deliveries, Loss of packet, No-response, No feedback etc. from any cargo company.

As a discipline of our parent organization, we are committed to render Class 1 Services to all our patronage.
& this has made us the ACE Choice for our Clients!

We believe the success of our company can be attributed to the way we approach this business. We strictly follow the principle that all our manpower should be our direct employees and not on contractual basis. They are carefully selected and properly trained in accordance with the changing transportation needs of our esteemed clients.
